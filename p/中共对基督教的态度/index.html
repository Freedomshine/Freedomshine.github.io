中共对基督教的态度

Mar 08, 2025

从圣经的角度来看，家庭教会更符合基督是教会元首的教导，因为它们通常不受政府的控制和干涉，能够更自由地实践和传播信仰。而三自教会，尽管在某种程度上能够合法地进行宗教活动，但由于受到共产党无神论政府的控制，许多人认为这与圣经的教导相悖。基督徒普遍认为教会应当由基督而不是世俗政权来领导，因此，从这个角度来看，家庭教会更符合圣经的教导。

From a biblical perspective, house churches align more closely with the teaching that Christ is the head of the church because they are generally not controlled or interfered with by the government, allowing them to practice and spread the faith more freely. On the other hand, the Three-Self Churches, despite being able to conduct religious activities legally to some extent, are controlled by the atheistic Communist government, which many believe contradicts biblical teachings. Christians generally hold that the church should be led by Christ rather than secular authorities, thus from this viewpoint, house churches more closely conform to the teachings of the Bible.

中国的基督徒受逼迫就是因为他们坚持基督是教会唯一的元首。

Christians in China are persecuted because they insist that Christ is the only head of the church.

共产党认为信仰是人类的精神鸦片（马克思主义观点），还有一些共产党认为，基督教是西方宗教，是对中国的文化入侵，是外部势力视图干预中国统治。因为中国最后一个王朝清朝主要是因为外国入侵而衰落灭亡，所以中共当局目前非常不希望自己的国民被外部干预，永永远远当皇帝，共产江山代代传给子孙。这就是中共的逻辑。他们不允许基督教传播，是担心失去手中的权力。

The Communist Party views faith as the opium of the people (a Marxist perspective) and some in the party believe that Christianity, being a Western religion, represents a cultural invasion of China and an attempt by external forces to interfere with Chinese sovereignty. Since the last Chinese dynasty, the Qing Dynasty, fell primarily due to foreign invasions, the current Chinese authorities are extremely reluctant to allow any external interference, aspiring to rule indefinitely and pass the Communist regime down through generations. This is the logic of the Communist Party. They prohibit the spread of Christianity because they fear losing their power.
