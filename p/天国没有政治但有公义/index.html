天国没有政治，但有公义

Mar 08, 2025

The kingdom of heaven has no communism, no Communist Party, no capitalism, no Liberal Party, no Conservative Party, no elections, no Democratic or Republican Party, no oppression and no oppressed, no politics at all. The kingdom of heaven doesn’t require a passport to enter; God is fully sovereign. There is no injustice. “From the days of John the Baptist until now the kingdom of heaven has suffered violence, and the violent take it by force” (Matthew 11:12, ESV). As Christians, I believe we are not called to be concerned with politics. What we truly care about is our relationship with God and whether we are obeying His will. Resisting evil should not be based on political balance, but rather on God’s Word.

We are first and foremost citizens of the Kingdom of Heaven, and only then are we Canadians, Americans, Chinese, or any other nationality. Our earthly birthplace and nationality certainly impact us greatly, but they pale in comparison to the influence of the Kingdom of Heaven. For passports, nationality, power, wealth, glory, and status are all worthless in God’s eyes; only a repentant heart and faith are precious to Him. What does it matter if we obtain Canadian citizenship? After a few short decades, we will pass away. “Vanity of vanities, all is vanity.” Yet we still face the challenges of this fleeting world with our frail bodies, eagerly awaiting the day of redemption.

Romans‬8:19-22 ESV For the creation waits with eager longing for the revealing of the sons of God. For the creation was subjected to futility, not willingly, but because of him who subjected it, in hope that the creation itself will be set free from its bondage to corruption and obtain the freedom of the glory of the children of God. For we know that the whole creation has been groaning together in the pains of childbirth until now.

Well said, brother. 2 Cor. 5:20 refers to Christians as AMBASSADORS for Jesus Christ, not diplomats who live lives of compromise, nor politicians who foresake the peace of their conscience in oder to please men and stay in power. How wonderful to know that we are citizens of a Kingdom that is not of this world (Jn. 18:36) and so is not subject to the whiles of conniving men and the whims of a fickle electorate. Hallelujah!!
