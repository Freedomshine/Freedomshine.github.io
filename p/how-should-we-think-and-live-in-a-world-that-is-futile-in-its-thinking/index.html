How Should We Think and Live in a World That Is Futile in Its Thinking

Mar 08, 2025

How Should We Think and Live in a World That Is Futile in Its Thinking?

In a world where many reject God and pursue futile thinking, we as Christians must stand firm in truth, live by faith, and be light in the darkness. The Bible provides clear guidance on how to navigate a world that denies God. Here’s how we should think and live in such a world:

1. Think Biblically, Not Worldly

“Do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that by testing you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect.”
Romans 12:2 (ESV)
“不要效法这个世界，只要心意更新而变化，叫你们察验何为神的善良、纯全、可喜悦的旨意。”
（罗马书 12:2, 和合本）

  • The world rejects God’s truth and replaces it with its own philosophies (such as atheism, humanism, and materialism).
  • We must renew our minds daily through Scripture and reject the world’s corrupt ways of thinking.
  • Practical Application: Read the Bible daily, meditate on Scripture, and test everything against God’s Word.

2. Live in Faith, Not in Fear

“For we walk by faith, not by sight.”
2 Corinthians 5:7 (ESV)
“因我们行事为人是凭着信心，不是凭着眼见。”
（哥林多后书 5:7, 和合本）

  • The world often promotes fear—fear of the future, fear of lack, fear of suffering.
  • But as Christians, we live by faith in God, trusting that He is in control.
  • Practical Application: When facing difficulties, pray instead of worrying. Trust that God will provide.

3. Proclaim the Gospel Boldly

“For I am not ashamed of the gospel, for it is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes.”
Romans 1:16 (ESV)
“我不以福音为耻；这福音本是神的大能，要救一切相信的。”
（罗马书 1:16, 和合本）

  • The world rejects the Gospel and often ridicules believers, but we must not be ashamed.
  • The only way people can be freed from futile thinking is through the power of the Gospel.
  • Practical Application: Share the Gospel with love and wisdom. Live in a way that makes people curious about Christ.

4. Be the Light in the Darkness

“You are the light of the world. A city set on a hill cannot be hidden.”
Matthew 5:14 (ESV)
“你们是世上的光。城造在山上是不能隐藏的。”
（马太福音 5:14, 和合本）

  • The world is full of moral corruption, deceit, and selfishness.
  • As Christians, our lives should reflect God’s holiness, love, and truth.
  • Practical Application: Show kindness to others, be honest in work and school, and help those in need.

5. Endure Suffering for Christ

“Indeed, all who desire to live a godly life in Christ Jesus will be persecuted.”
2 Timothy 3:12 (ESV)
“凡立志在基督耶稣里敬虔度日的，也都要受逼迫。”
（提摩太后书 3:12, 和合本）

  • This world is against God, so we should expect opposition.
  • But suffering for Christ is a sign that we are truly following Him.
  • Practical Application: Do not fear rejection. Stand for truth, even when it’s unpopular.

6. Set Your Mind on Eternity

“Set your minds on things that are above, not on things that are on earth.”
Colossians 3:2 (ESV)
“你们要思念上面的事，不要思念地上的事。”
（歌罗西书 3:2, 和合本）

  • The world chases after money, power, pleasure, and success.
  • But as believers, we must remember that this world is temporary, and our true home is in heaven.
  • Practical Application: Instead of chasing earthly success, focus on storing up treasures in heaven by serving God and loving others.

7. Stay in Fellowship with Other Believers

“And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another.”
Hebrews 10:24-25 (ESV)
“又要彼此相顾，激发爱心，勉励行善。你们不可停止聚会，好像那些停止惯了的人，倒要彼此劝勉。”
（希伯来书 10:24-25, 和合本）

  • In a world that is against God, we cannot stand alone.
  • We need fellowship, encouragement, and accountability from other believers.
  • Practical Application: Join a Bible-believing church, attend fellowship meetings, and build relationships with godly friends.

Conclusion: How Should We Live?

In a world full of futile thinking, we must:

  1. Think biblically and renew our minds in God’s Word.
  2. Live by faith, trusting in God’s sovereignty.
  3. Proclaim the Gospel and share the hope of Christ.
  4. Be the light by living a holy and loving life.
  5. Endure suffering with courage and patience.
  6. Set our minds on eternity, not earthly desires.
  7. Stay in fellowship with other believers for encouragement.

The world may reject God, but we are called to live differently, to shine brightly, and to stand firmly in the truth of Jesus Christ.

“Do all things without grumbling or disputing, that you may be blameless and innocent, children of God without blemish in the midst of a crooked and twisted generation, among whom you shine as lights in the world.”
Philippians 2:14-15 (ESV)
“凡所行的，都不要发怨言，起争论，使你们无可指摘，诚实无伪，在这弯曲悖谬的世代作神无瑕疵的儿女；你们显在这世代中，好像明光照耀。”
（腓立比书 2:14-15, 和合本）
