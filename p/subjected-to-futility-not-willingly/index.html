Subjected to futility, not willingly

Mar 08, 2025

​Hi, Ben,

I wish yesterday’s spiritual conversation, especially the part about China did not give you a negative influence.  I wish I could share with you more biblical thoughts in my perspective.

I believe that as the economy declines,  violent crime rates tend to rise in a society under high-pressure governance. I would like to call China a social Darwinism Society because we were brainwashed to believe it. 

The authorities educate the public that the wealth of the rich comes from their hard work totally, promoting the idea of “winner takes all” while claiming the poor deserve to be poor and serve the rich and power.   A senior CCP general even remarked, “If you want regime change, be prepared 30 million heads for exchange.” This reflects an extremely terrifying reality: sin profoundly influences this world. Actually, all humans are slaves of sin. The whole world system is Slavery, ruled by sin and satan.

It also highlights the difficulty of acquiring resources; not everyone is born in countries like Canada or the United States. Many are born into extreme poverty and under authoritarian regimes. For many, their fate may have been determined at the moment of their birth. They are unaware that they are destined to fail in the competition of this secular world.
