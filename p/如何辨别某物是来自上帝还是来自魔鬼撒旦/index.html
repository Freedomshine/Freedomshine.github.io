如何辨别某物是来自上帝还是来自魔鬼撒旦

Mar 08, 2025

Discerning whether something is from God or from the devil can be a challenging but crucial aspect of spiritual life. Here are some key principles to help you discern:辨别某物是来自上帝还是来自魔鬼，这可能是属灵生活中一个具有挑战性但至关重要的方面。以下是一些关键原则，可帮助您辨别：

Biblical Alignment

  1. Scripture Consistency: God’s guidance will always be consistent with His Word, the Bible. If something contradicts Scripture, it is not from God.圣经的一致性：上帝的引导将永远与他的话语，圣经保持一致。如果某件事与圣经相矛盾，那它就不是来自上帝。
    • Verse Reference: “All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness” (2 Timothy 3:16, ESV).经文参考：“圣经都是神所呼出的，对教导、责备、纠正、训练公义都是有益的”（提摩太后书3：16）。

Fruits of the Spirit圣灵的果子

  1. Spiritual Fruits: Evaluate the fruits of the action or message. If it promotes love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control, it is likely from God.属灵的果实：评估行动或信息的果实。如果它促进爱、喜乐、和平、忍耐、仁慈、良善、信实、温柔和自制，它很可能是来自上帝的。
    • Verse Reference: “But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control; against such things there is no law” (Galatians 5:22-23, ESV).经文参考： “但圣灵所结的果子是仁爱、喜乐、和平、忍耐、恩慈、良善、信实、温柔、节制;没有律法可以抵挡这种事“（加拉太书 5：22-23）。

Inner Peace

  1. Peace: God’s guidance often brings a sense of peace and assurance, even in difficult situations. Satan’s influence, conversely, often leads to confusion, fear, and unrest.平安：即使在困难的情况下，上帝的引导也常常带来平安和保证的感觉。相反，撒但的影响往往会导致混乱、恐惧和不安。
    • Verse Reference: “And let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts, to which indeed you were called in one body. And be thankful” (Colossians 3:15, ESV).经文参考： “让基督的平安在你们心中掌权，你们确实被召为一体。你们要感恩“（歌罗西书3：15）。

Prayer and Seeking Counsel

  1. Prayer: Seek God in prayer and ask for His wisdom and discernment. The Holy Spirit can provide clarity and insight.祷告：在祷告中寻求上帝，祈求他的智慧和辨别力。圣灵可以提供清晰和洞察力。

    • Verse Reference: “If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask God, who gives generously to all without reproach, and it will be given him” (James 1:5, ESV).经文参考： “你们中间若有人缺乏智慧，就当求神，他慷慨地赐给众人，无可指责，就必赐给他”（雅各书 1：5）。

  2. Godly Counsel: Seek advice from mature, godly individuals who are grounded in their faith. They can offer wise counsel and help discern God’s will.敬虔的忠告：向以信仰为基础的成熟、敬虔的人寻求建议。他们可以提供明智的建议并帮助辨别上帝的旨意。

    • Verse Reference: “Where there is no guidance, a people falls, but in an abundance of counselors there is safety” (Proverbs 11:14, ESV).经文参考：“没有指引的地方，百姓就跌倒，但有众多的谋士就有安全”（箴言 11：14）。

Testing the Spirits

  1. Test the Spirits: The Bible instructs believers to test the spirits to see whether they are from God.试探灵：圣经指示信徒试探灵，看它们是否来自上帝。
    • Verse Reference: “Beloved, do not believe every spirit, but test the spirits to see whether they are from God, for many false prophets have gone out into the world” (1 John 4:1, ESV).经文参考： “亲爱的，不要信各样的灵，要试探灵，看它们是否来自神，因为有许多假先知已经到世上去了”（约翰一书 4：1）。

Motivation and Outcome动机和结果

  1. Motivation: Assess the motivations behind the action or message. God’s guidance is motivated by love, truth, and righteousness, while Satan’s influence is often marked by selfishness, deceit, and sin.动机：评估行动或信息背后的动机。上帝的引导是出于爱、真理和公义，而撒旦的影响往往以自私、欺骗和罪恶为标志。

Conclusion

By aligning your discernment process with these principles, you can better determine whether something is from God or from the devil. It is important to stay grounded in prayer, Scripture, and the counsel of trusted believers.通过使你的辨别过程与这些原则保持一致，你可以更好地确定某件事是来自上帝还是来自魔鬼。重要的是要扎根于祷告、圣经和可信赖信徒的忠告
